About Gleedoc

Based in Seoul, Korea, Gleedoc is a premier medical illustration studio dedicated to creating accurate, detailed, and visually compelling medical artwork. Our team combines artistic expertise with deep medical knowledge to produce illustrations that serve healthcare professionals, educators, and researchers worldwide.

We specialize in creating precise anatomical illustrations, surgical procedure diagrams, pathological representations, and medical device technical drawings that meet the highest standards of accuracy and clarity.
